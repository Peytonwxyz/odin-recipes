Marla's Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin cookies, pumpkin bars, pumpkin lattes, pumpkin cupcakes, pumpkin bread, but HELLO what about pumpkin pie?? My Great Pumpkin Pie Recipe is here today.

Did you know that testing the perfect pumpkin pie recipe is a lot more challenging than one would assume!? Fresh pumpkin, canned pumpkin, ginger, no ginger, brown sugar, white sugar, cornstarch, flour, no cornstarch, no flour…?

But I finally I cracked the pumpkin pie code. I grew up in a house of pumpkin pie lovers and absolutely no Thanksgiving was complete without a nap and a massive slice of pumpkin pie. What I’m trying to say is, my pumpkin pie standards are high for this classic Thanksgiving dessert.-Sally's Baking Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Libby's pumpkin pie filling
  • 1 Pie Crust
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1.25 cups of brown sugar
  • 1 TableSpoon Corn Starch
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground cinnamon/li>
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie seasoning
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 0.25 cups milk

Directions

  1. Preaheat oven to 375
  2. Insert chilled pie crust into 12 inch pie pan
  3. Whisk the pumpkin, 3 eggs, brown sugar & season together until combined. Whisk vigorously
  4. Pour Pumpkin pie filling into the crust
  5. Bake for 60 minutes
  6. Serve warm or chilled with whip cream
